Stay Hydrated

  • Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily.
  • Helps with digestion and skin health.
  • Be Mindful of Caffeine & Alcohol
  • Avoid sugary drinks for better hydration.
  • Eat Water-Rich Foods

Balanced Diet

  • Eat more fruits and vegetables.
  • Limit processed and sugary foods.
  • Include healthy fats and proteins.
  • Get Enough Fiber for digestion and gut health
  • Eat at regular intervals to keep metabolism steady

Exercise Regularly

  • At least 30 minutes of activity daily.
  • Choose Activities You Enjoy
  • Incorporate strength training
  • Set Goals & Track Progress
  • Include a Mix of Exercises

Get Enough Sleep

  • Aim for 7-9 hours per night
  • Make Your Bedroom Sleep-Friendly
  • Avoid screens before bedtime
  • Avoid Caffeine & Heavy Meals Late at Night
  • Limit Naps During the Day
